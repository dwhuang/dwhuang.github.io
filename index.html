About Me

I am a PhD student working with Jim Reggia on biologically-inspired computing, with a focus on neural computation. The goal is to build adaptive and intelligent software by borrowing functioning principles from the brain.
I received my MS and BS in Computer Science & Information Engineering from National Taiwan University in 2006, and National Chiao-Tung University in 2004, respectively. My master's thesis was on wireless mesh networking.
