About Me

I am a PhD candidate working with Jim Reggia on neural computation with a focus on self-organizing maps. I'm working on a dissertation about self-organizing map architectures based on attractor dynamics. I'm also working as a research assistance for an ONR project about robot imitation learning and mental simulation.
In addition to neural computation, I'm interested in other forms of bio-inspired AI, especially evolutionary computation and swarm-intelligence.
I like to program, for research purposes or not.
I received my MS in Computer Science and Information Engineering (CSIE) from National Taiwan University. My master's thesis was on wireless mesh networking. Before that, I received my BS in CSIE from National Chiao-Tung University.
